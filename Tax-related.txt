* ITR(Income Tax Return) - Income tax return is a form to inform the government about the income a person or organization or company earned during a financial year.

* ITR is a legal proof of income

* There are 7 types of ITR forms like ITR-1, ITR-2,....,ITr-7

The income may comes from salary/business/property/fixed deposit/lottery etc.,

* Generally Indian financial year means the period from 1st April to 31st March

* According to the income tax act, anybody who's age is less than 60 years and has annual income of more than 2.5Lakhs they has to file ITR.

For the senior citizens the limit is 3 Lakhs
For people who has more than 80 years of age the limit is 5 Lakhs

* India has 'Progressive system tax' - Less tax on less income & more tax on more income

* Taxes are 2 types
	1) Direct tax
	2) Indirect tax
		-> Service tax
		-> VAT (Value Added Tax)
		-> CST (Central Sales Tax) - Transporting goods from one state to another state

The ideal option would be to invest Rs 1.5 lakh between April 1 and April 5 (total limit for investing in a year is Rs 1.5 lakh) at the start of the financial year.
* An individual can deposit money into a PPF account, a maximum of 12 times, during a given financial/fiscal year. Also, No more than two deposits can be made to PPF scheme, during any given month
* Any transaction over and above this amount should ideally be rejected by the bank.
* Extend the PPF account by five years without further contributions: This option allows you to extend your account maturity by 5 years. That is, the corpus will continue to earn interest. And you don't have to make any fresh contributions during this extended five-year period
